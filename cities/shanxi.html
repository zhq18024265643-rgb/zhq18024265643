Shanxi is a treasure trove of ancient Chinese architecture and Buddhist culture, with over 20,000 ancient buildings dating back 1,500 years. It is the real inspiration for the hit game Black Myth: Wukong, with stunning grottoes and temples that mirror the game's magical world.

Hanging Temple

A miraculous temple built into a cliff 1,500 years ago, suspended by wooden beams with no nails. A blend of three religions and iconic Black Myth scenery.

Yingxian Wooden Pagoda

The oldest wooden pagoda in the world, built without nails over 900 years ago - a masterpiece of ancient engineering.
