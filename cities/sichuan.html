Sichuan - Land of Pandas & Spicy Delights

ğŸ“œ History Introduction

Sichuan Province, located in southwest China, is known as the "Land of Abundance" for its fertile plains and rich natural resources. It has a long history of over 2,000 years, with a unique culture, beautiful natural scenery and world-famous Sichuan cuisine. It is the hometown of giant pandas and one of China's most popular tourist destinations.

ğŸï¸ Top Attractions

Chengdu Panda Base

Home to over 100 giant pandas and red pandas, a must-visit for animal lovers. Visitors can watch pandas eat, play and sleep in their natural habitat, and learn about conservation efforts for this endangered species.

Jiuzhaigou Valley

A UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its colorful alpine lakes, waterfalls and snow-capped mountains. The crystal-clear blue and green lakes change color with the light, creating a fairy-tale landscape.

Mount Emei & Leshan Giant Buddha

Mount Emei is a sacred Buddhist mountain with stunning views and wild monkeys, while the Leshan Giant Buddha is a 71-meter tall stone statue carved into a cliff face, one of the largest Buddha statues in the world.
