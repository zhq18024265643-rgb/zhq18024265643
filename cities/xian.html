Xi'an, formerly Chang'an, is one of China's Four Great Ancient Capitals, serving 13 dynasties for over 3,100 years. It is the starting point of the Silk Road and the birthplace of Chinese civilization, home to countless ancient relics.

Terracotta Army

The Eighth Wonder of the World, an underground army of thousands of clay soldiers built for Qin Shi Huang. A UNESCO Heritage Site and a breathtaking ancient masterpiece.

Xi'an City Wall

China's most complete ancient city wall, built in the Ming Dynasty. Cycle along the wall for panoramic views of the old town and modern city.

Giant Wild Goose Pagoda

A Tang Dynasty Buddhist pagoda built to store scriptures, surrounded by a beautiful park and musical fountain.
