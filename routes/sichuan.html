Sichuan 7D6N Complete Travel Itinerary

Best of Sichuan: Chengdu ¬∑ Jiuzhaigou ¬∑ Leshan ¬∑ Mount Emei

Day 1 & 2 - Chengdu (Capital of Sichuan)

Morning: Visit Chengdu Panda Research Base to see giant pandas. Afternoon: Walk in Jinli Ancient Street & taste local snacks. Evening: Enjoy authentic Sichuan Hot Pot & relax in a tea house.

Day 3 & 4 - Jiuzhaigou Valley

Explore the colorful alpine lakes & waterfalls. Visit Huanglong Scenic Area with travertine pools. Stay in a mountain hotel & enjoy starry night views.

Day 5 - Leshan Giant Buddha

Visit the 71m tall stone Buddha statue. Boat tour on the Minjiang River to see the Buddha's full view. Taste Leshan's famous braised duck & tofu pudding.

Day 6 & 7 - Mount Emei

Hike to the Golden Summit for sunrise & sea of clouds. Meet wild monkeys in the mountain forest. Visit ancient Buddhist temples & enjoy vegetarian food.
